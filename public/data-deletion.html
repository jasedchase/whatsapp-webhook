Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 30, 2026

WhatsApp AI ChatBot provides users the right to request deletion of their personal data.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to jasedchase@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include your WhatsApp phone number and a brief request

Processing Time

We will process deletion requests within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

Confirmation

You will receive confirmation once your data has been deleted.
